Wind Release: Flashing Fireworksedit

風遁・閃光華美­ (Fūton: Senkō Hanabi)

Offensive

Range

Short to Mid range (0-10m)

The user throws a handful of shuriken into the air like one would throw flowers. With a swing of their Giant Folding Fan, the user then encloses the shuriken in wind, making them hit the floor and spring back up in several sequences.
