Scorch Release (灼遁, Shakuton, Viz: Scorch Style) is a nature transformation kekkei genkai. When Sasuke Uchiha and Naruto Uzumaki combine their fire and wind-natured techniques, the resulting technique is dubbed "Scorch Release", suggesting those are its component natures.

Pakura of Sunagakure uses Scorch Release to evaporate all the water inside her target’s body, leaving them as a desiccated corpse. In the anime, her Scorch Release is weak against Water Release.
